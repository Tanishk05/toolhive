## Utility products need content primitives

Posts alone do not make a content strategy. You need categories, tags, authors, and related posts so the system can guide the reader.

## Editorial structure improves utility

When readers can move from a post to a category, then to a tool or author page, the content behaves like a navigable product surface.

## Content should support the product

Use the blog to answer questions the product cannot answer in a card or a tool page.

### Good targets

- implementation notes
- strategy walkthroughs
- launch updates
- SEO explainers
