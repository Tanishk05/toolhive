## Canonical URLs are part of the contract

Every blog route should know its canonical URL before it renders. That keeps metadata deterministic and simplifies sitemap generation.

## Structured data should mirror reality

When the page has an author, category, and date, the JSON-LD should reflect those fields directly. Do not invent extra data that the UI cannot verify.

### Recommended layers

1. Page metadata
2. Article JSON-LD
3. Breadcrumb JSON-LD
4. RSS feed
5. Sitemap entries

## Keep the source single

One registry should feed:

- the blog index
- the article page
- author profiles
- category archives
- tag archives
- RSS
- sitemap.xml
